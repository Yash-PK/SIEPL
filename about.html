Your trusted partner in Oil and gas EPC solutions

Established in 2008 and headquartered in Navi Mumbai, Spectrum Innovative Engineers Pvt. Ltd. (SIEPL) is a leading provider of Engineering, Procurement, and Project Management solutions for the global energy sector. We specialize in delivering high-quality equipment, systems, and services for both offshore and onshore oil & gas operations, in partnership with world-renowned multinational companies. Backed by a leadership team with over 35 years of experience, SIEPL is known for its technical expertise, on-time delivery, and strict adherence to HSE and quality standards.
