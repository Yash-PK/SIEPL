High-performance industrial solutions

Centrifugal Pump

Centrifugal pumps transport fluids by converting rotational kinetic energy into hydrodynamic energy, enabling efficient and continuous fluid flow. Designed to move liquids through a rotating impeller, these pumps deliver consistent flow and pressure, making them ideal for water supply, chemical processing, oil & gas, wastewater treatment, and more.

Centrifugal Pump

Reciprocating Pump

Reciprocating pumps are positive displacement pumps designed for high-pressure, low-flow applications requiring precision and reliability. Using a piston or plunger mechanism, these pumps deliver a fixed volume of fluid with each stroke, making them ideal for applications where accurate dosing or consistent pressure is essential.

Reciprocating Pump

Vacuum Pump

Designed for demanding applications such as solvent dewaxing, wastewater treatment, and additive/lubricant production. These systems provide efficient solid-liquid separation, continuous operation, and high reliability in critical industrial processes.

Vacuum Pump

Centrifugal Compressor

Centrifugal compressors are high-performance machines designed to efficiently compress gases by converting kinetic energy into pressure energy through dynamic rotary action. Ideal for continuous, high-flow applications, they are widely used in industries such as oil & gas, petrochemicals, power generation, and air separation.

Centrifugal Compressor

Reciprocating Compressor

Reciprocating compressors are positive displacement machines designed to deliver high-pressure gas compression with precision and reliability. Using a piston-driven mechanism, these compressors are ideal for applications requiring continuous compression of air, natural gas, or other industrial gases.

Reciprocating Compressor

Screw Compressor

Screw compressors are reliable, energy-efficient solutions for continuous gas and air compression. Utilizing twin intermeshing rotors, screw compressors deliver smooth, pulse-free flow, making them ideal for a wide range of industrial applications.

Screw Compressor

Liquid Ring Compressor

We offer liquid ring compressors capable of delivering discharge pressures up to 15 bar abs. Engineered for durability and efficiency, these compressors feature low energy consumption, minimal maintenance requirements, and optimal performance in continuous-duty applications.

Liquid Ring Compressor

Emergency Diesel Generator

Emergency Diesel Generator

Natural Gas Processing Units

We offer a custom design and provide comprehensive natural gas processing solutions designed to meet industry standards and client-specific requirements. Our services cover the full spectrum of gas treatment and conditioning, like GDU, Glycol regeneration etc.

1. Natural Gas Processing Units

Glycol Dehydration Units remove water vapor from natural gas using TEG technology, ensuring reliable long-term dehydration in harsh environments.

Glycol regeneration systems to strip the absorbed water, salts, and contaminants from the Rich MEG to produce Lean MEG that can be recycled to storage tanks and production wells. Regeneration units help maintain dehydration efficiency, reduce chemical losses, and extend equipment life.

Molecular sieve dehydration is the preferred technology over glycol dehydration where very low water dew points are required.

Gas Sweeting is solutions to remove acid gas components (CO2 and / or H2S) from natural gas.

Dew Point Control Units (DPCU) are designed to inhibit the formation of solid hydrates in gas streams

Fuel Gas Treatment packages are used to provide specific pre-treatment of gas feed streams. FGTP consist of Knock out drums, filter, Heaters, Gas metering / condensate handling, Gas storage system, Emergency shutdown panel.

Separators are used to separate liquid, gas and water produced from Hydrocarbon wells.

The technology enables CO2 to be selectively absorbed from flue gas via contact with a regenerable solvent, which is specially design to selectively absorb CO2 from gas streams.

Effluent/Produced Water Treatment

Water treatment systems are designed to remove oil and other contaminants from produced water or effluent, treating it to meet the required standards for safe disposal or potential reuse.

2. Effluent/Produced Water Treatment

Deoiler hydrocyclones are highly efficient devices used to remove and recover oil from produced water and other process streams, ensuring effective separation and improved water quality.

Gas flotation is a well-established method for oil recovery in water treatment applications, utilizing bubble generation technology and Compact Flotation Units (CFUs) to enhance separation efficiency.

Filtration systems are widely used for the removal of liquids—such as water and hydrocarbon condensates—from gas streams to ensure product purity, protect downstream equipment, and enhance overall process efficiency.

Corrugated Plate Interceptor (CPI) units are designed to remove free oil and suspended solids from water as a primary stage in the treatment process, using inclined plate packs as the main separation mechanism.
